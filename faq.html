Monuments in Leiden - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most famous monuments in Leiden?

Leiden is home to several famous monuments, including the Burcht van Leiden, the Pieterskerk, and the Waag.

How can I visit these monuments?

You can visit these monuments during their opening hours. Many of them offer guided tours.

Are there any entry fees for the monuments?

Some monuments may have an entry fee, while others may be free to visit. It's best to check their official websites for details.

What is the history behind the Burcht van Leiden?

The Burcht van Leiden is a historic castle that dates back to the 10th century and offers stunning views of the city.

Can I take photos at these monuments?

Photography is generally allowed, but some places may have restrictions, especially inside.
